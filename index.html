About us

ELK Moving is a professional and reliable organization based in Calgary offering a wide range of moving services to both residential and commercial clients.
Our mission is to provide high-quality and fast moving services in accordance with the expectations of our clients

We understand that moving can be a stressful and challenging process, which is why we strive to make it as easy and comfortable as possible for our clients.

Our team of professional and experienced workers is committed to ensuring the safe and hassle-free transportation of all your belongings. We pack and secure your items, carefully load them, transport them and unload them to your new home or office, meeting all deadlines and requirements.

Why choose ELK Moving?
Regardless of where you are moving from or to, our goal is to ensure the safe transportation of your home comfort and convenience.
>1000 satisfied clients
Each and every call and client is very important for us. We treat every order with the utmost priority and importance, whether it is a large move or a smaller transportation of 1-3 items.
Wide range of services
ELK Moving company carries out moves of any complexity. Assembling and disassembling furniture, packing and unpacking items, installing electrical appliances and household appliances and much more
Saving time and effort
Our experts know how to pack and move your belongings in a proper and organized manner to reduce the time you would have spend on this process. We work with professional tools and use special techniques that make it easier to carry furniture and other heavy objects.
Cargo transportation safety
Our professionals know how to properly pack and secure products in transport so that they are not damaged during transportation. Our company is also responsible for protecting furniture and interior items from scratches, loss and other damage.
